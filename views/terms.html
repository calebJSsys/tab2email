Terms of Use & Transparency

Last updated: February 2026

What is TabMail?

TabMail is a small, non-commercial, personal-use web application built to help friends and family send guitar chord sheets to a loved one serving as a missionary who has email access but cannot freely browse the internet.

Non-Commercial & Non-Profit

This project is not operated for profit. There is no revenue, no advertising, no data collection, no analytics, and no tracking. It was built as a personal tool out of love for a family member and is shared only with a small group of friends and family.

How It Works

Users search for guitar chord sheets, which are fetched from publicly available sources on the internet. The chord sheet is converted into a clean PDF and emailed directly to the missionary. That's it — search, generate, send.

Chord Sheet Sources

Chord sheets displayed and sent through TabMail are sourced from publicly available guitar tab websites. TabMail does not host, store permanently, or claim ownership of any chord sheets or lyrics. All content is the property of its respective authors and rights holders. If you are a content owner and have concerns, please reach out and we will promptly address them.

Data & Privacy

  • No accounts. Access is via a single shared password — we don't collect names, emails, or personal data from users of the web interface.
  • No database. There is no persistent data store. Search results are cached briefly in memory and cleared when the server restarts.
  • No tracking. No analytics, cookies (beyond the session cookie for login), or third-party scripts.
  • Emails. The only email address stored is the recipient's (the missionary), configured server-side by the administrator.

Fair Use

This application is intended for personal, non-commercial use within a small private group. It sends a small number of chord sheets per day and operates at minimal scale. We believe this falls within fair use for personal, educational, and non-commercial purposes.

No Warranty

This tool is provided "as-is" with no warranties of any kind. It may stop working at any time if upstream sources change. It is a personal project, not a commercial service.

Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or are a rights holder with a takedown request, please contact the site administrator directly. This is a family project and we will respond promptly and in good faith.
